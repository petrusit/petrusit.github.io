What makes us different

We will not create software which makes you dependent upon us for maintenance, further development or support. Our use of Open Source technology means that other developers can modify, support or extend our solutions. We are confident however, that our work will speak for itself and that our clients will freely choose to come back to us.

We also believe that trust should be earned, and therefore we structure our projects to create solid client benefits at each step along the way. Clients take one step at a time and keep going only as long as they feel that they are continuing to get good value for their investment. We don't ask our clients to bet their entire budget at the beginning.

Our basic principles

  • The client should always be part of the team.
  • Use solid software engineering practices.
  • Keep up to date with technological advances.
  • Utilize appropriate scientific methods.
